Pizza Deliciozo

Description

This is a delicious pizza coming right from the oven!

A tasty pizza choice which chicken and sprinkled with quattro fromagi on top!

Ingredients

The necessary ingredients are the following:

  • 1-1/2 pounds bulk Italian sausage
  • 3 cups uncooked rigatoni or large tube pasta 4 cups shredded part-skim
  • mozzarella cheese 1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • undiluted 1 small onion
  • chopped 2 cans (one 15 ounces, one 8 ounces) pizza sauce
  • 1 package (3-1/2 ounces) sliced pepperoni
  • 1 can (6 ounces) pitted ripe olives, drained and halved

Steps to make

  1. In a skillet, cook sausage until no longer pink; drain. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
  2. In a 5-qt. slow cooker, layer half of the sausage, pasta, cheese, soup, onion, pizza sauce, pepperoni and olives. Repeat layers. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours.
